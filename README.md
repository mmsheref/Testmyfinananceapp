# Aysha's P&L Dashboard

Aysha's P&L Dashboard is a comprehensive, mobile-first web application designed to help restaurant owners track their daily profit and loss with ease. It provides a simple and intuitive interface to manage daily sales, categorize expenses, and gain insights into financial performance.

## Key Features

*   **Dashboard Overview**: Get an at-a-glance view of your financial health with key metrics like 7-day and 30-day average profit, a visual profit/loss trend chart, and a list of recent activity.
*   **Daily Record Management**: Easily create, view, update, and delete daily financial records. Each record captures total sales and a detailed breakdown of expenses for a specific day.
*   **Detailed Expense Tracking**: Log expenses across predefined and custom categories (e.g., Market Bills, Meat, Labours, Fixed Costs). The app intelligently pre-fills recurring costs from your most recent entry to speed up data entry.
*   **Customizable Expense Items**: Add new, custom expense items on the fly and save them for future use, tailoring the app to your specific business needs.
*   **Bill Photo Upload**: Attach a photo of a bill to any expense item for easy record-keeping and verification. Images are automatically compressed for efficient storage.
*   **Data Visualization**: A simple bar chart on the dashboard visualizes your profit and loss over the last 30 records, helping you spot trends quickly.
*   **Search and Filter**: Quickly find past records using the date-based search functionality in the records list.
*   **Shareable Reports**: Generate a clean, shareable image of any daily record's detailed breakdown using the "Share" feature.
*   **Data Backup & Restore**: Safeguard your data by exporting all records into a single JSON file. You can easily restore your data from a backup file at any time, ensuring you never lose your financial history.
*   **Mobile-First & Responsive**: Designed with a mobile-first approach for a seamless experience on your phone, but also fully functional on tablets and desktops.
*   **Offline First**: All data is stored locally in your browser, so the app works perfectly even without an internet connection.

## How to Use

1.  **Open the App**: Launch the application in your web browser.
2.  **Add a Record**: Tap the `+` button to create a new entry for the day.
3.  **Enter Data**: Fill in the total sales and go through the expense categories to input your daily costs.
4.  **Save**: Once done, save the record.
5.  **Analyze**: Use the dashboard to see your performance over time or view detailed reports for specific days.
6.  **Backup**: Regularly use the "Export Data" feature in the Settings menu to back up your records.
